7. Diagnosed the **"cron isn't working" report**: it actually was running, but (a) the Gmail message fetch was capped at 20 with no pagination, so it could never reach more than the 20 newest cockpit-labelled emails, and (b) historical Imperial data had genuinely been corrupted by bug #4 above. Fixed the cap (20 → 150) and did a full clean re-ingest for Imperial (deleted last 30 days of its rows across 3 tables, re-ran the Gmail check, verified every day came back correct and reconciled).
