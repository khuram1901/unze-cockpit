Reflow & new components

Desktop → Mobile
01 · NEW
Systems health tile
Compact card with title + status pill (OK / Degraded / Down), display-font metric, mini sparkline, and a one-line meta note. Reusable for any live-status dashboard (bank feeds, integrations, servers).
02
Reuses HR patterns
Tinted KPI cards, Create-task form with priority-in-select, and accent-ruled suggested cards — all lifted directly from HR. Different content, same components.
03
Suggested from live data
Suggestions surface from two sources: current systems health (e.g. "Lahore IP is down → create task") and meeting minutes decisions (e.g. "Implement live IP attendance"). Reduces the empty-state to actionable next steps.
04
Split form + suggestions
Desktop shows the Create-task form (1.5fr) beside a stack of suggested tiles (1fr) — click a suggestion to pre-fill the form.
05
Mobile: systems as 2×2 mini-KPIs
Systems health tiles collapse to a 2×2 grid using the m-kpi shell, with the status pill shrunk to 9.5px. Sparklines drop on mobile — they'd be too small to read.
06
Dark mode ready
Every component uses design-system tokens, so this page (and every other one) instantly reflects dark mode when the toggle in the sidebar is switched on.
