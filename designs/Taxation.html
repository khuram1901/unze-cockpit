Reflow & new components

Desktop → Mobile
01 · NEW
Entity-grouped list
Notices are grouped by entity with a tinted group header (letter mark + name + count + total amount). Reusable for any list that needs "by owner" hierarchy — invoices by customer, PRs by vendor, etc.
02 · NEW
Hero KPI in row
Last KPI in the row is inverted (dark ink-900 background, white text) to signal it's the summary of the previous four. Reused when one KPI naturally rolls up the others.
03 · NEW
Amount "TBD" state
Where the tax liability isn't yet quantified, "Amount TBD" appears in muted ink-400 instead of a red zero — signals "unknown" vs. "confirmed zero". A general treatment for indeterminate figures.
04
Trio chart row → single donut
Desktop shows Entity donut + Type donut + Exposure bars. Mobile keeps only the Entity donut on the summary; other charts live behind a "Detail →" link.
05
4-col rows → 2-line cards
Desktop notice rows are 4-col (title, amount, status, cta). Mobile: title on top full-width, then a bottom row with type badge + hearing date on the left, amount on the right.
06
Group amount in header
Desktop group headers show "5 pending · 5 total · PKR 84.2M". Mobile promotes the amount to the section-head link position on the right (in danger red) — most valuable info at a glance.
