Reflow & new components

Desktop → Mobile
01 · NEW
Meta row
A compact top bar with Plant + Date selectors and quick actions (Copy from yesterday, Submit all). Sticks to the top of the form on wider screens.
02 · NEW
Progress step row
4 side-by-side cards showing sub-form status. Warm-amber tint = "needs input"; grey = "optional / nothing to report"; dark disc = "done". Reusable for any multi-part form.
03 · NEW
Entry card (form column)
Each sub-form is a standalone card with title, hint, steppers, optional PO/status fields, and a footer with Submit + "Nothing to report" text-link. All 5 sit in a 5-col grid on desktop.
04 · NEW
Past entries row
Compact 6-col table row: date · produced · dispatched · broke · status pill · action. Broken counts > 0 tint warning to draw attention without shouting.
05
5 cards → 1 at a time (tabs)
Desktop shows all 5 forms side-by-side. Mobile shows one form at a time via a horizontal tab strip (with warning dots on tabs that still need input), plus a step-list summary below.
06
"Nothing to report" pattern
Instead of forcing a user through every sub-form, each card shows a subtle text link. One tap marks that sub-form complete without data — much friendlier than the current hidden "Nothing to report" state.
